Elite Athlete Support Program

As part of our on-going elite athlete support program, Riverside are delighted to partner with Mona McSharry in her goal to achieve Olympic qualification. Mona’s recent Gold in the World Junior Swimming Championships in Indianapolis adds to her national senior record in the 100m breaststroke and her collection of gold and silver medals in the 50m, 100m and 200m at the European Juniors earlier this summer. We are proud to support such a world class Irish Athlete and we wish Mona the best of luck for the European Swimming Championship in Copenhagen this December. More updates on Mona’s progress and on other athletes in this program, will follow.
